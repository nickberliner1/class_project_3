Watch It Outside has been going strong for the last 15 years

Watch It Outside’s mission is to develop opportunities for diverse audiences to discover film through creative, thought-provoking experiences. Since 2004, we have worked to promote film as both an art form and a civic forum. We’re dedicated to engaging both our members and the general public in a lifelong relationship with film.

Don't believe us? Join us this year in Boston and see for yourself. We can guarantee you will find people who are just as interested, engaged, and passionate about new films that you are.
